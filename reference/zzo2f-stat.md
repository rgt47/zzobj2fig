# Statistical-Object Helpers for the Typst Backend

Thin adapters that extract tabular content from fitted models or other
statistical objects and route it through
[`zzo2f()`](https://rgt47.github.io/zzobj2fig/reference/zzo2f.md).
Parallel in purpose to
[`o2f_tidy()`](https://rgt47.github.io/zzobj2fig/reference/o2f_tidy.md),
[`o2f_coef()`](https://rgt47.github.io/zzobj2fig/reference/o2f_coef.md),
[`o2f_rms_compare()`](https://rgt47.github.io/zzobj2fig/reference/o2f_rms_compare.md).
